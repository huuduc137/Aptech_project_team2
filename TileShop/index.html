Cera - Tiles of World

Tiles opened its first store in 1963 in Sale, Manchester and then continued to open stores across the North West and the Midlands. In 1990, Cera-Tiles of World merged with Tile Kingdom and consequently expanded its presence into the South and London. Cera-Tiles of World continued to grow following the merger and became a public limited company (PLC) in 1997.
